LabAid is a daily management system for the laboratory that takes all the manual, handwritten processes of your day-to-day operations and conveniently captures them in one application.

From documenting temperatures and reagent changes to tracking inventory and expiration dates, LabAid is your one-stop shop for efficiently capturing and storing your data, removing the need for archving and maintaining paper storage.
